Collect with Indelibl

Why choose Indelibl?

Indelibl provides art to all sorts of businesses, from interior designers to restaurants and hospitals. By collaborating with curators and designers, our bespoke approach will meet your needs and budget. Whether it’s a commission, a curated selection from our catalogue, or a wholesale purchase you’re after, we’ve got you covered.

For Collectors

Indelibl serves as a discovery and art collection management tool for art collectors. Our members only collectors club is an exclusive network of first time and established collectors who share one passion: art. The main goal of the club is to enrich the relationship between collectors and artists and to build a support system which empowers collectors to collect and artists to be collected confidently. Our vision is to conscientiously guide the use of art collecting as a tool not only of consumption but also of education and promotion. Membership to the CC gives access to a diverse range of programs and activities in the burgeoning african art scene.
